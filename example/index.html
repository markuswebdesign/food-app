About Us

A holistic approach to gut health & nourished living. At Greenive, we believe that true wellness begins with understanding the deep connection between your gut, your mind, and your daily habits.

Every body is unique — and so is every healing journey. That’s why we create personalized nutrition strategies tailored to your needs, your pace, and your goals. Whether you’re struggling with digestion, low energy, inflammation, or simply seeking a more natural lifestyle, we guide you with clarity and compassion every step of the way.

Greenive is more than a coaching experience. It’s a space to reconnect with yourself, build healthier habits, and discover the nourishment your body truly deserves.

Our Programs & Services

Personalized Programs for Holistic Wellness

1. Gut Health Reset Program

This program focuses on identifying root causes of bloating, inflammation, and digestive discomfort. Through mindful nutrition, gentle lifestyle adjustments, and digestive support strategies, you’ll begin to heal your gut naturally.

2. Holistic Nutrition Coaching

Together, we explore your habits, food patterns, energy levels, and emotional connections with food. You’ll receive ongoing support, tailored strategies, and clarity to help you build a nourishing relationship with your body.

3. Energy Boost & Detox Program

This program helps eliminate toxins, reduce fatigue, and support liver + digestive health using natural whole foods and hydration practices—no harsh detoxes or extreme diets.

4. Mindful Eating Workshop

This workshop teaches you to tune into your body's cues, reduce emotional eating, and create a calmer, more intuitive approach to meals.
